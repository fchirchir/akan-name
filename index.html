Discover Your Akan Name!

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

This is a web application that takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born and prints out your Akan Name based on your gender.

Fill the form below and select your gender to find out your Akan Name.
